# 🎬 AI vs Humanity — Cinematic Storytelling Experiment

A short cinematic project exploring one of the most important questions of our time:

👉 *Will AI replace humans… or will humans evolve with AI?*

---

## 🚀 Concept

This project tells a powerful story in just **2 scenes**:

### Scene 1 — Replacement
A factory transitions from human workers to full automation.
The protagonist loses his job and walks away.

### Scene 2 — Evolution
The same person adapts, learns, and returns — now leading AI instead of being replaced by it.

---

## 🎯 Core Message

> “Adaptation is survival.”

---

## 🧠 Tools Used

- 🖼️ Nano Banana — Image generation
- 🎥 FLOW — Image-to-video cinematic generation with voice synthesis and Ambient+Cinematic scoring
- Utilised Extend functionality in FLOW to scene 2 from Scene 1 with prompt engineering technique

---

## 🎬 Prompt Engineering

This project uses **structured cinematic prompting**, combining:

- Camera motion
- Lighting progression
- Emotional arcs
- Narration timing
- Music design
